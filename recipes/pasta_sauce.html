THE Sauce

About this recipe

While this isnt entierly "home made" this is a quick and easy way to dress up a store bought sauce. Turn that $4 jar into a pot of golden poasta sauce with this recipe.

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup prepared mincemeat, crumbled
  • 1 (28 oz) jar prepared pasta sauace
  • 1/2 cup tomato puree, or to taste (Optional)
  • 5 leaves frsh basil, chopped

Directions

  1. In a pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat, and cook and stir the onion until slightly browned, about 10 minutes. Stir in the mincemeat, and cook and stir until mincemeat is hot and well mixed with onion. Pour in the pasta sauce and tomato puree, and mix in the basil leaves. Bring the sauce to a simmer, and cook until the flavors have blended, about 10 minutes. Stir often.
